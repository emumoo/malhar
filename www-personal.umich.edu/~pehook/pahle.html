इससे पहले कि हमें देख लेते ...
  Anterior clauses, compound verbs, and mood.

            Clauses introduced by इससे पहले कि ... are used to express actions and events that are preceded or prevented by a prior action or event expressed in the main clause. In (1) for example the event of someone getting angry is preceded in time by the speaker's leaving the scene:

1. इससे पहले कि उसे क्रोध आता, हम जल्दी से खसक लिए।                             [2290dee.blogspot.com]
    'We lit out of there before he got angry.'

In (2) the action of the waves is prevented altogether by a boy's intervention:

2. इससे पहले कि गेंद सामने से आती लहर की लपट में चली जाती, उसने टखने-टखने पानी में जाकर उसे पकड़ लिया।           [मवाली by मोहन राकेश]
    'Before an incoming wave could carry the ball away he ran into the shallow water and caught it.
